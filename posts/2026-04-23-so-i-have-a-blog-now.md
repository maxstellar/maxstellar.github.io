I'm not quite sure what to write in it. I remember at the beginning of this process of making a blog (or at least, asking Claude to make a blog) that I had something I wanted to write about, but to be honest, I'm not quite sure I remember what it is. Maybe it was about a project I was interested in? An idea that I had about Hack Club? Whatever it was, I can't remember.

Often times in life, I find myself entering rooms, opening DMs, typing in URLs, and then forgetting what I came there to do. It's frustrating. My mother would have you believe it's a result of my phone--but to be honest, she says that for everything.

Perhaps there's an art to mindfulness; it's good to be aware of where you are, what you are doing, who you're with, all of that. But I also find a strange sort of romanticism to just going with the flow, being a wanderer who floats from space to space until he remembers where he is.

Either way, this has gone on a bit too long for my first blog. It was only intended to test if deployments actually worked. Hopefully, they do!
Thanks for reading.
